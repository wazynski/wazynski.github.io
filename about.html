A designer & full stack developer with over 9 years experience. I enjoy working with a diverse range of clients. Planning, designing and developing, extraordinary brands, websites, and apps.

I’m also a keen photographer, although I spend far too much time taking photos and never using them. Yet, I do give away them away royalty free on Unsplash.

Previously, I was the lead innovation developer at Lancaster University, where I helped to start the Innovation Hub. Before that, I was a design partner at Single Malt and a co-founder and lead developer of Porter's Coffee.
